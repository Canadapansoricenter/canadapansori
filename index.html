About

Canada Pansori Center is dedicated to preserving and promoting the traditional Korean art of Pansori and related performing arts in Canada. Our mission is to create a bridge between cultures through music, storytelling, and performance.

Programs

  • Pansori Vocal Training
  • Korean Traditional Percussion (Samulnori)
  • Korean Traditional Dance
  • Workshops and Performances
