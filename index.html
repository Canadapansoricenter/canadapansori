About

Canada Pansori Center is dedicated to preserving and sharing the traditional Korean art of Pansori, traditional dance, and percussion in Canada. Through performance and education, we connect generations and cultures.

Programs

  • Pansori Vocal Training
  • Samulnori Percussion
  • Korean Traditional Dance
  • Workshops & Performances
